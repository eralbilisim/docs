## Why Engage with Our Community?

*   **Stay Informed:** Be the first to receive official announcements, roadmap updates, and news about partnerships and HPAi development.
*   **Direct Interaction:** Opportunities to engage directly with the HealthPass.run core team and advisors.
*   **Learn & Share:** Participate in discussions about healthcare innovation, AI, blockchain technology, and the HealthPass.run ecosystem.
*   **Provide Feedback:** Your insights and suggestions are valuable. Our community channels are a place to share your thoughts and help shape the future of the project.
*   **Network:** Connect with like-minded individuals, healthcare professionals, tech enthusiasts, and potential collaborators.
*   **Exclusive Opportunities:** Information on NFT mints, potential $HPT token pre-sales or airdrops, and community-specific events will often be shared first through these channels.

We are committed to fostering a respectful, constructive, and enthusiastic community. We look forward to welcoming you!
